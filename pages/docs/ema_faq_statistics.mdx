## EMA FAQs: Statistics (Source: https://www.ema.gov.sg/resources/faqs/statistics/electricity/what-is-the-national-average-monthly-household-consumption-for-electricity)

What is the national average monthly household consumption for electricity?
You may refer to our annual Singapore Energy Statistics (SES) publication for the average monthly consumption for electricity (T3.4 and T3.5) and town gas (T3.8 and T3.9) by household types and planning area.


## EMA FAQs: Statistics (Source: https://www.ema.gov.sg/resources/faqs/statistics/electricity/how-is-electricity-demand-forecasted)

How is electricity demand forecasted?
Electricity demand forecast accounts for various factors, including changes to population, temperature, projected Gross Domestic Product (GDP), and projected demand from new high-growth sectors such as data centres. You may also refer to the Singapore Electricity Market Outlook (SEMO) for more details.


## EMA FAQs: Statistics (Source: https://www.ema.gov.sg/resources/faqs/statistics/electricity/where-can-i-find-singapore-energy-statistics-eg-residential-electricity-consumption-data)

Where can I find Singapore energy statistics, e.g. residential electricity consumption data?
You may refer to our annual Singapore Energy Statistics publication (SES) which consists of an interactive microsite and downloadable data tables. For residential electricity and town gas consumption data, you can refer to Chapter 3 of our SES microsite or refer to the downloadable data tables (Table 3.4 to 3.5 for electricity consumption and Table 3.9 to 3.11 for town gas consumption). 
You may also visit the Statistics section for the list of energy statistics published by EMA.


## EMA FAQs: Statistics (Source: https://www.ema.gov.sg/resources/faqs/statistics/electricity/what-is-singapores-historical-and-forecasted-electricity-demand)

What is Singapore's historical and forecasted electricity demand?
You may refer to our annual Singapore Energy Statistics publication (SES) for the historical electricity demand. For forecasted electricity demand, visit the Singapore Electricity Market Outlook (SEMO) for the details.


## EMA FAQs: Statistics (Source: https://www.ema.gov.sg/resources/faqs/statistics/gas/what-is-the-national-average-monthly-household-consumption-for-gas)

What is the national average monthly household consumption for gas?
You may refer to EMA’s annual Singapore Energy Statistics (SES) publication for average monthly town gas consumption by household types and planning area (T3.8 and T3.9).


## EMA FAQs: Statistics (Source: https://www.ema.gov.sg/resources/faqs/statistics/carbon-emissions/what-is-grid-emission-factor-gef)

What is Grid Emission Factor (GEF)?
The Grid Emission Factor measures the amount of carbon emissions per unit of electricity generated. This includes all generation sources such as natural gas, solar and waste-to-energy. To view the latest energy statistics, visit our Statistics section.
