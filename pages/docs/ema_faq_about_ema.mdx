## EMA FAQs: About EMA (Source: https://www.ema.gov.sg/resources/faqs/about-ema/ema-scholarships/what-is-the-criteria-for-applying-for-a-scholarship-with-ema)

What is the criteria for applying for a scholarship with EMA?
To apply for our scholarship, you should fulfil the following criteria:

A Singapore Citizen or Permanent Resident
Have achieved good academic results in the following:
GCE ‘A’ Level
International Baccalaureate (IB)
Polytechnic Diploma
University results (for mid-term scholarship application)


Possess outstanding records in CCA and other non-academic activities



## EMA FAQs: About EMA (Source: https://www.ema.gov.sg/resources/faqs/about-ema/ema-scholarships/do-i-need-to-secure-a-university-offer-before-applying-for-scholarship)

Do I need to secure a university offer before applying for the scholarship?
You are not required to secure a university offer before sending in your application. However, if your application is successful, you will be required to provide proof that you have accepted a university offer.


## EMA FAQs: About EMA (Source: https://www.ema.gov.sg/resources/faqs/about-ema/ema-scholarships/can-i-apply-for-the-scholarship-after-starting-my-undergraduate-studies)

Can I apply for the scholarship after I have started my undergraduate studies?
You can still apply for our scholarship after commencing your undergraduate studies. However, you need to complete at least one year of studies and submit your results as part of your application.
In the event that your application is successful, the bond period and benefits that you enjoy will be pro-rated.

When can I apply for the scholarship?
The application window for our scholarship usually opens after the GCE ‘A’ Level results are released.
Please visit this page for the most up-to-date information on the application cycles.


## EMA FAQs: About EMA (Source: https://www.ema.gov.sg/resources/faqs/about-ema/ema-scholarships/when-should-i-apply-for-scholarship-if-i-am-due-to-enlist-for-national-service)

When should I apply for the scholarship if I am due to enlist for National Service?
You can submit your application after receiving your GCE ‘A’ Level results.
Alternatively, you can apply for the scholarship while serving your National Service. Please note that no deferment of National Service will be granted if your application is successful.

## EMA FAQs: About EMA (Source: https://www.ema.gov.sg/resources/faqs/about-ema/ema-scholarships/what-does-the-scholarship-selection-process-involve)

What does the scholarship selection process involve?
If you are shortlisted, you will undergo several rounds of selection, which may include interviews, activity-based assessments, and psychometric tests.


## EMA FAQs: About EMA (Source: https://www.ema.gov.sg/resources/faqs/about-ema/ema-scholarships/when-will-i-know-if-i-am-shortlisted-for-the-scholarship)

When will I know if I am shortlisted for the scholarship?
After the scholarship application window closes, we will review the applications. We aim to notify all shortlisted applicants progressively by end-March.


## EMA FAQs: About EMA (Source: https://www.ema.gov.sg/resources/faqs/about-ema/ema-scholarships/what-is-the-bond-period-of-the-ema-scholarship)

What is the bond period of the EMA scholarship?
The bond period is 4 years for local studies and 6 years for overseas studies. For mid-term scholars, the bond period will be pro-rated.


## EMA FAQs: About EMA (Source: https://www.ema.gov.sg/resources/faqs/about-ema/ema-scholarships/what-happens-if-i-choose-to-end-the-bond-period-prematurely)

What happens if I choose to end the bond period prematurely?
If you decide to break the bond, you will be required to repay liquidated damages, which include the scholarship award amount with annually compounded interest.


## EMA FAQs: About EMA (Source: https://www.ema.gov.sg/resources/faqs/about-ema/ema-scholarships/can-i-continue-post-graduate-studies-after-my-university-studies)

Can I continue my post-graduate studies if I were to pursue it immediately after my undergraduate studies?
You may pursue your post-graduate studies (i.e Masters) after your undergraduate studies. However, the sponsorship of your post-graduate studies will be subject to approval and scholars’ fulfilment of certain criteria.


## EMA FAQs: About EMA (Source: https://www.ema.gov.sg/resources/faqs/about-ema/ema-scholarships/what-career-opportunities-will-be-available-upon-starting-work)

What career opportunities will be available to me upon starting work after completing my studies?
As part of our scholarship program, our scholars have access to various development opportunities, such as job rotations across different work areas in EMA, secondments to other agencies and provision of technical and management training.
You will also get to intern with EMA during one of your term breaks to gain greater work exposure and experience. The internship usually occurs at the end of your first or second year of undergraduate studies.


## EMA FAQs: About EMA (Source: https://www.ema.gov.sg/resources/faqs/about-ema/ema-scholarships/what-will-be-the-starting-salary-after-joining-ema-upon-completion-of-my-studies)

What will be the starting salary after I join EMA upon completion of my studies?
Your starting salary will be determined based on your qualifications and experience. We are committed to offering competitive salaries that keep pace with the market.
